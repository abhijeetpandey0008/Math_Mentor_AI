PROBABILITY FORMULA

P(A) = Favorable outcomes / Total outcomes

RANGE

0 ≤ P(A) ≤ 1

COMPLEMENT RULE

P(A') = 1 - P(A)

ADDITION RULE

P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B)

INDEPENDENT EVENTS

P(A ∩ B) = P(A) P(B)

CONDITIONAL PROBABILITY

P(A|B) = P(A ∩ B) / P(B)

BAYES THEOREM

P(A|B) = [P(B|A) P(A)] / P(B)

TOTAL PROBABILITY

P(A) = Σ P(A|Bi)P(Bi)
